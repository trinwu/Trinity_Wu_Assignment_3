Jellyfish Animation

Please use a browser that supports the <canvas> element.

Instructions

  • Rotate: Click and drag on the canvas to rotate the jellyfish (trackball rotation).
  • Poke: Hold Shift and click on the canvas to make the jellyfish cry (poke animation).
  • Adjust: Use sliders to control the body angle and tentacle joints individually.
